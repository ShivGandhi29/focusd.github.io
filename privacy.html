Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Focusd ("we," "our," or "us"). Focusd is a free to-do list application designed to help you stay focused and get things done. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Focusd is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide you with the app's functionality:

  • Task Data: The tasks, notes, and related information you create within the app are stored locally on your device.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app's performance and user experience.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information (such as device type and operating system version) to ensure compatibility and optimize performance.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • Improve and optimize the app's performance
  • Ensure compatibility with your device

Data Storage

Your task data is stored locally on your device. We do not store your personal tasks or data on our servers. This means:

  • Your data remains private and secure on your device
  • We cannot access your personal tasks or information
  • If you delete the app, your data will be removed from your device

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. We do not:

  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your data for marketing purposes

Third-Party Services

Focusd may use third-party services (such as analytics tools) that help us understand how the app is used. These services may collect anonymous usage data but do not have access to your personal task information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored in the app
  • Delete your data at any time by deleting the app
  • Control what information you share within the app

Children's Privacy

Focusd is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
